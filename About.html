Ultrasonic Cavitation

Ultrasonic Unoisetion Cavitation + Radio Frequency (RF) is a non-invasive alternative to liposuction which uses ultrasonic waves to turn fat cells into free fatty acids, which are easier for the body to get rid of. With cavitation, micro-bubbles are created in the fat cells, which weakens & liquefies fat, releasing fat cell contents to be reused or eliminated by the body naturally through the urinary tract and lymphatic system.

Non-Invasive

Unlike surgical liposuction there is no pain, incisions, or recovery time. Ultrasonic energy is delivered through the skin surface. The treatment is safe and suitable for men and women who want effective removal of excess fat without surgery. Cavitation is a good option for those who want to smooth, tighten, tone, and reduce cellulite - within realistic expectations. 

Lose inches

At least 6-12 sessions are recommended for best results. Ultrasonic cavitation is not a weight loss treatment, it is a circumferential reduction procedure. This means that you won’t be able to measure your success by how much fatty tissue you’ve lost, but instead by how many inches you’ve shrunk in your treatment area. Diet and exercise is highly recommended during treatment. 

Newest Technology

We use unoisetion cavitation which is the newest and most intelligent technology available. It provides 30% reduction of the "tingy" noise in your ears during treatment. Normal cavitation feels hot, but unoisetion cavitation does not feel hot as the energy is directly aimed at the derma fat cell.
