FAQ - Cavitation

How does Ultrasonic Cavitation work?

Ultrasonic Cavitation destroys fat cells permanently with low frequency ultrasound which creates bubbles in the liquid surrounding fat cells by causing vibrations that the fat cells do not have the structural capacity to withstand so they burst.

How does Radio Frequency work?

Radio Frequency energy meets resistance in the tissue, the generated heat is deposited into the dermis causing collagen contraction & regeneration, improving wrinkles & laxity. Thermal effects of RF energy also reduce cellulite & excess body fat.

What happens to the released fat?

Ultrasonic cavitation causes emulsification of the fat cells and eliminates the fat through the urine and the lymphatic system.
Water intake is key for this elimination process.  

How long is a treatment?

Our treatment sessions are 45-min per area. On the body we use ultrasonic cavitation & radio frequency. On the face and neck we use bio-microcurrent & radio frequency and sessions are 60-min.  You can get up to 3 treatments in one day on different areas.  

What will I feel during the treatment?

We use the newest unoisetion technology which reduces the “pitch” heard during cavitation. During RF, there is a slight amount of heat, so we keep the wand in constant motion which keeps it comfortable. Please let your technician know if you are feeling any discomfort and he/she will adjust accordingly.

Is it painful?

No, our sessions are non-invasive and painless.  Occasionally post treatment might result in some slight mild redness, but it does not cause pain. The heat is perfectly tolerable and most clients find it quite comfortable and relaxing - several clients even fall asleep :)

Is this a safe treatment?

Yes, Ultrasonic Cavitation, Radio Frequency and Biocurrent are non-invasive & non-surgical procedures without anesthesia, which allows for a full social life both before and after the sessions, with no side effects. 

Is this FDA approved?

Yes. Ultrasonic Cavitation was the first and only FDA-cleared, non-invasive body-shaping procedure to use focused, pulsed ultrasound energy to selectively destroy fat cells, and was FDA approved in the United States in 2011.

What if I need to use the restroom during treatment?

 No problem! This is expected as we ask you to drink plenty of water in preparation for your session. We keep a spa robe on hand for quick restroom breaks, just let your technician know and he/she will pause your treatment for you. 

How often can I get a treatment or treatments?

Every 2 days. Your body needs time to eliminate the fat.  For example:  If you have a treatment on Tuesday, you can come back on Thursday or Friday.  We recommend 1 or 2 times per week until you reach your expectations. 

Can this be performed on both men & women?

Yes, of course!
Our treatments can most definitely benefit both males and females alike. We all have the desire to feel better in our own skin!  And, please note that our technician is a male.

Is it possible to lose weight with Ultrasonic Cavitation?

Ultrasonic cavitation is used to SHAPE the body, it is not a method to lose weight. The treatment reduces stubborn fatty pockets that just won’t go away with diet and exercise. The way your clothes fit is a great way to "measure" your success.

How many sessions are usually needed?

Many clients appreciate results from the third or fourth session, yet we recommend at least 6 and up to 12 sessions for better results, with upkeep every so often after your initial treatments. *Please note that not all clients will have the same results.

Do you take before & after photos?

No, to maintain a timely schedule, we do not take before and after photos, or take measurements. But we encourage you to do so on your own and would love for you to share your photos and success with us if you'd like!

FAQ - Red Light Therapy

Who can use a red light therapy bed?
Red light therapy is entirely safe and non-invasive, meaning it can be used by anyone –
young or old and up to 400 lbs.  

What is the standard treatment like?
Similar to lying in a tanning bed, but red light therapy beds don't have UVA or UVB rays, so you won't feel as if you have a sunburn after treatment and is completely safe and beneficial.
The light is simply used for up to 20-min per session.  No lotion is required.  

Can I wear makeup in the Red Light Therapy bed?
Yes, makeup can be worn and should not affect the performance of the therapy.

Can I wear my clothes in the Red Light Therapy bed or should I be nude?
The skin that you wish to be treated should be fully exposed to the light.  You can choose to wear something or go completely nude, it's up to you.  

How often can it be used?
The red light would ideally be used 2-3 days per week to start off and continue like that for 4-6 weeks, but it is safe for daily use.  For general maintenance and health once per week or once every other week is acceptable.

Are there any risks from red light therapy?
There are no serious side effects documented.

Do I need to wear some sort of eye protection?
It is not required that you wear eye protection, nevertheless the lights are very bright and may be uncomfortable.  We provide goggles for you to use, should you choose to. 

What are the benefits of red light therapy and what can it do for me?
Effective in treating skin conditions, wrinkles, promoting collagen, wound healing, hair growth, skin damage, pigmentation, alopecia, eczema, carpal tunnel, arthritis, promotes circulation, reduce inflammation, chronic pain, increases blood flow, acne, cystic acne, fibromyalgia, chronic fatigue, depression & anxiety, improves eye & oral health, thyroid function, stimulate metabolism, encourage weight loss, ease tendinosis and tendonitis pain, tattoo healing, bone healing, and so much more.  

What are the aftercare recommendations?
Plenty of fluids.  Make sure you stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
